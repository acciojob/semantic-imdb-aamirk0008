Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film based on Stephen King's novella. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man sentenced to life in prison for a crime he didn't commit, and how he finds hope and redemption through acts of common decency.

Actors

Main cast members include:

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton

Quotes

One of the most memorable quotes from the movie:

"Hope is a good thing, maybe the best of things, and no good thing ever dies."
