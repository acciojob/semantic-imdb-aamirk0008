Plot Summary

Set thirty years after the events of the original Blade Runner, Officer K, a young blade runner, discovers a long-buried secret that has the potential to plunge what's left of society into chaos. K's discovery leads him on a quest to find Rick Deckard, a former LAPD blade runner who has been missing for 30 years. This neo-noir science fiction film explores themes of identity, humanity, and what it means to be "real" in a world where the line between human and artificial intelligence becomes increasingly blurred.
