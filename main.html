Plot Summary

Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a skilled thief, the best in the dangerous art of extraction: stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state.

Cobb is unable to visit his children, and is offered a chance to regain his life if he can accomplish the seemingly impossible task of inception: the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Cobb's team must navigate through multiple layers of dreams within dreams, facing projections from their target's subconscious and Cobb's own haunting memories.
